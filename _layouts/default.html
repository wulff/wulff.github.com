In Norse mythology, Ratatosk ("Sharptooth") is a squirrel who runs up and down the world tree Yggdrasil to carry messages between the unnamed eagle, perched atop Yggdrasil, and the wyrm Nidhogg, who dwells beneath one of the three roots of the tree. Wikipedia
